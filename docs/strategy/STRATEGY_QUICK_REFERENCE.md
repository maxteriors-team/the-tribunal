## 💡 Key Insights

### Why This Works When Other CRM Plays Don't

1. **You're Not Selling Software**
   - Software = commodity, price pressure
   - Outcomes (appointments) = premium, no price competition

2. **Risk Reversal is Underutilized**
   - Most B2B vendors ask customers to take all the risk
   - When you take the risk, you stand out immediately

3. **Moats > Features**
   - Features get copied (Salesforce, HubSpot have bigger teams)
   - Data, community, and exclusivity can't be copied

4. **Start Narrow → Go Wide**
   - "CRM for everyone" = CRM for no one
   - "AI appointment engine for roofers" = clear winner

5. **Speed Wins**
   - Perfect plan executed slowly loses
   - Imperfect plan executed fast wins
